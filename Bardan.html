Dear Students and Parents/Guardians,
I am honored and feel extremely privileged to be the Center Head of the Paragon Public School, Junior Wing. On behalf of the entire school fraternity, I welcome you to the Paragon Public School Family.
As Center Head, I am hugely impressed by the commitment of the school and the staff to the provision of an excellent all-round education for our students in our state of the art facilities. As a team working together, we strongly promote academic achievement among our students. The cultural, sporting, and other successes of all of our students and staff are also proudly celebrated together.
We take seriously the trust you place in us by sending your children to our school every day, and we work hard to make students and families feel safe and supported. Our staff is planning engaging and rewarding learning experiences for all students. We believe that each and every student has a gift to share and that we can help nurture that gift.
Paragon Public School is an innovative institution drawing on the talents and skills of staff and students to provide a host of educational programs and projects. Wholesome participation is encouraged in the extensive range of extra-curricular activities on offer and care is also taken to ensure the well-being and happiness of each and every student in the school.
Looking back at our previous school years we have much to celebrate and be thankful, for our staff, students and parents helped our school to earn the recognition and appreciation that we have been yearning for. As great as the last school year was for Paragon Public School, the new school year has the potential to be even better. To do this, your continued help, cooperation, and support will be needed to reach greater heights.
We are looking forward to another amazing school year in service to our students, parents, and community. I wish you all a wonderful and productive school year.
With a long and rewarding history of achievement in education behind us, our school community continues to move forward together with confidence, pride, and enthusiasm.
Prasenjit Bardhan
Center Head (Junior Wing)
