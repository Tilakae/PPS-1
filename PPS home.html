Welcome to Paragon

-About Us

Paragon Public School was established in the year 1994 in Battisputali, Kathmandu with 57 students and 15 staff members. In the year 2003 with the vision of providing quality education at an affordable cost by using modern pedagogies in the state-of-the art infrastructure facilities it shifted to its own property of around 15 ropanis land in Tilganga, Kathmandu which is in heart of the city. It has modern academic buildings with spacious classrooms, laboratories, Activity hall, separate boys and girls hostel buildings, administrative building, dining hall equipped with modern kitchen facilities.
